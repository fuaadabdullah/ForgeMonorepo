## Team Coordination Guidelines

### For Facilitators

1. **Prepare Thoroughly**: Review agenda and materials beforehand
2. **Keep on Time**: Manage time boxes and transitions effectively
3. **Encourage Participation**: Ensure all voices are heard
4. **Focus on Outcomes**: Drive toward actionable decisions
5. **Follow Up**: Ensure action items are clear and assigned

### For Participants

1. **Come Prepared**: Review materials and agenda in advance
2. **Be Present**: Minimize distractions and multi-tasking
3. **Contribute Actively**: Share relevant information and insights
4. **Listen Attentively**: Pay attention to others' contributions
5. **Follow Through**: Complete assigned action items on time

### For Note Takers

1. **Capture Accurately**: Record key points, decisions, and action items
2. **Organize Clearly**: Use consistent formatting and structure
3. **Highlight Action Items**: Make ownership and due dates clear
4. **Distribute Promptly**: Share notes within 24 hours
5. **Archive Properly**: Store in appropriate knowledge base location

### Meeting Types Guide

**Daily Standup** (15 minutes):

- What did you do yesterday?
- What will you do today?
- Any blockers?

**Sprint Planning** (2-4 hours):

- Review sprint goal and capacity
- Select and estimate backlog items
- Create sprint backlog and commitment

**Sprint Review** (1-2 hours):

- Demonstrate completed work
- Gather feedback from stakeholders
- Update burndown and metrics

**Retrospective** (1-2 hours):

- What went well?
- What could be improved?
- What actions will we take?
